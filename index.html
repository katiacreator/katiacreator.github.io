ABOUT ME

Hello World! My name is Katia Gibson, and I am an aspiring fullstack web developer and UI/UX designer. As a recent graduate of Temple University, my academic background is in medical sociology, specifically secondary data analysis of existing CDC data related to race/ethnicity and ethnographic observation.

I have over 10 years of professional experience providing case management services and database management for various nonprofits assisting minority populations increase their access to health resources and education, as well as speaking at academic conferences about issues related to reproductive health and LGBTQ issues.

I am interested in the concept of futuristic archives and seek to use code to become a virtual museum curator. My long-term goal is to marry my passion for public health education/advocacy for minority populations and my love for creative storytelling.
